The Challenge

Why Distributors Struggle with Efficiency

Malaysian distributors face growing complexity in sales tracking, inventory management, and demand planning — leading to missed opportunities and costly overstock.

⏰

Blind Inventory Decisions

Without real-time stock visibility, distributors overstock slow-movers and run out of best-sellers — costing margins and customers.

🔄

Disconnected Sales Data

Sales trends buried across spreadsheets and AutoCount reports make it nearly impossible to spot opportunities quickly.

📉

Reactive, Not Proactive

Without demand forecasting, distributors are always reacting — restocking after stockouts and discounting after overstock piles up.
